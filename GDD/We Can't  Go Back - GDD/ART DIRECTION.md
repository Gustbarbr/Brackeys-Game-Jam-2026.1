
* **COLOR DIRECTION:** The game transitions from a warm, sepia-toned palette to a desaturated and cold palette as the photo deteriorates. Red is used sparingly to represent emotional warmth at first, and later violence and loss.

* **VISUAL STYLE:** Digital Art;

* **PALETTE**
	* **Warm Palette:**
		* Old beige (photo background) → #D8CFC4
		* Light sepia (skin/aged paper) → #BFA58A
		* Soft brown (clothing/light shadow) → #8C6A4A
		* Dark brown (outline/frame) → #4A3626
		* Warm red (emotional detail) → #A23A2E
	* **Dark Palette:** 
		* Dark gray (background/empty) → #2B2B2B
		* Dirty medium gray (skin/damaged paper) → #6A6A6A
		* Bluish gray (uniform/coldness) → #4F5B63
		* Broken black (shadows/edges) → #141414
		* Opaque red (blood/damage) → #7A1E1E

* **COLOR USAGE**
	* Warm colors only in the first and second photo;
	* Dark colors on the third and fourth photos;
	* Red used in the first two photos as detail, and in the third and fourth photos as blood and damage;

* **SYMBOLISM**
	* **Heart:** life, humanity;
	* **Photo:** memory, identity, personal history;
	* **Enemies:** abstract representations, not literal.
