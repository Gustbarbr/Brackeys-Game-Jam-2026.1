**This level represents inevitable death before a stronger enemy and the sadness of not returning home.**

**Design Intent:** The player falls to someone stronger, representing the indistinguishable and inevitable nature of death in war.

It **appears** immediately **after** completing **[[THE THIRD LEVEL]]**. This level **only displays** the **soldier’s final words** and the **final photo**.

After the **player’s death**, the **fourth photo** appears with the phrase:  
**“Sorry, Mom. I won’t be coming back.”**

After the **player clicks** on the screen, the screen **turns black** and **displays** the **final message**:  
**“HQ: Deploying a substitute for Soldier 1402…”**
