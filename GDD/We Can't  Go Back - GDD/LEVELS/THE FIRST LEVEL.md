**This level represents hope of returning home and innocence.**

**Design Intent:** The absence of action represents tranquility, and the act of signing the document reinforces the idea of cold bureaucracy and a sealed fate.

It **appears** after the **player clicks** on the screen **during the showcase** of the **first phrase** of the game:  
**“Don’t worry, Mom. I’ll be back soon.”**

The document initially appears **blank**. After the **button is clicked**, a generic name, such as **John Doe**, appears. After the **player clicks** on the screen again, the **second photo** appears with the phrase:  
**“Yes, sir!”**
